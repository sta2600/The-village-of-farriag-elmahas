Holydays celebrations

The village has two celebration which are:

  • Eid Al-Adha
  • Eid Al-Fitr


Eid Al-Adha

This Eid is celebrated on the village by preparing spices, and the goats, lambs, or beef. Most of the people of the village who live outside the village, like to celebrate this Eid at the village with their family, so the village becomes full of the people from all over Sudan and outside of the country. That is why it makes a lot of people hold their wedding celebrations on the days of Eid Al-Adha, so that everyone can attend. The day of the Eid, women prepare the grilled meat with chili peppers and onion, beside the breads that has been baked specially for this day. In addition, they make a drink from dates they name it Sharboat, which helps to digest the meat and makes the stomach feel good.
