culture of Wedding Ceremony


This image is showing the culture of wedding ceremony.

The video is showing how men dance to celebrate a wedding

The wedding celebrations have many stages of celebration which begin a month before the wedding night. First the bride begins preparing herself for this night by buying clothes, perfumes, body and hair care products, and many other things. Then the bride’s family prepares perfume and mixes mixtures that are used for massage, peeling the body perfumed clothes and rooms. Girls gather every day in the bride’s house where they dance and sing. A week or two weeks before the wedding day, the family of the groom and the family of the bride begin to prepare many kinds of pastries and sweets to receive guests, and of course all members of the village help in every ceremony. Then, two days before the wedding, during singing and people of the village gather, henna is placed on the hands and feet of the groom, while the groom’s friends are content to put henna only on their hands. On the same day a professional woman who know all about henna comes and put henna on the hands and feet of the bride. The night before the wedding, they prepare for the wedding meal, which is beef meat and other dishes that are prepared from vegetables. Finally, the day of the wedding, the bride appears in a white dress with the groom in the middle of singing the people of the village.
