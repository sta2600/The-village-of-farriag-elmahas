Culture of harvest season

there are many tipes of harvest the the people of the village growing during the year, but the most important ones are the date harvest season and the wheet and fafa peans season


the date harvest season

Palm trees are harvested in August until the end of september of each year. The indigenous people of the village from all other states of Sudan attend the season of harvesting dates.


the wheat and Fafa beans season

As for Fafa beans and wheat, it is one of the most important crop on which the population depends. The cultivation of beans and wheat begins in september of each year and harvest is done after four months of cultivation.
