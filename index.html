Location and Information About the Villag of Farriag

Location of the Village

about the location of the village of Farriag Elmahs is located in far north of Sudan, it is bordered by some other Nubian villages from the north such as the village of Gedi and Sabo, and from the south, like Mashakila and Nouri. In addition, from the east there is the Nile River and from the west there are mountains.

Information about the Village of Farriag Elmahas

Some information about the Village of Farriag Elmahas is that Farriag is characterized by its ancient Nubian origins, where the Nubian language is the first language in the village and then the Arabic language. The village depends on seasonal agriculture, such as growing beans, wheat, and some fruits and vegetables. In addition to the palm trees that are harvested once a year. Besides agriculture, there are some few manuals works and trade that help add some income. There are some Government institutions and services such as the hospital of Farriag, the court, and the local management of Farriag and the villages around Farriag. Also, there is one public High School and another one that is elementary school. When it comes to the residential buildings, they are built from mud and palm trees.
