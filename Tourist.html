Tourist sides in Farraiag

The village of Farriag has many sides of tourist, which are

  1. Desert and Mountains nature.
  2. Agricultural land
  3. Archeological buildings(arn)

Desert nature

as the village of Farraig surrounded by a mountain desert nature, this nature give the village special a picturesque view that combines the desert nature and the banks of the Nile River.


Agricultural land

Since most of the population depends on agriculture as a primary source of income, this has made the banks of the Nile River beautiful Landscape surrounded by wheat spiles that swing with the winds and the high palm trees. In addition, there are some rocks that help to form waterfalls that add a beautiful view of the area of Farraiag village.

Archeological buildings(arn)

The village of Farraig contains a lot of buildings that were built a long time ago. These buildings are characterized by an old style. Some of this building is the dome of the Sheikh, Taj Alkhtim. This area “The Dome of the Sheikh Taj Alkhtim” of the village is sacred and has a great history that most people in the village of Farriag know about.
