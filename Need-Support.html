The Basics That Need Support in The Village

Because of the late entry of the technology in the village, many social and educational establishments need support. Some examples of these establishments

  • Health facilities
  • Educational facilities
  • Transportations

The health facilities

there is one hospital in Farriag that all the villages around Farriag attended to receive treatments. Although, the hospital of Farriag has a one doctor only and few nurses, a lot of patients visit it. The hospital needs beds and medicines, even the building of the hospital is not ready and inappeasable. However, the entire hospital is working with the support of parents.


educational facilities

There are two school in the village of Farriage, Farriage elementary school, and Farriag High School. The school need teachers and resources. Besides, the students need splices for schools. Although the high school building is still strong as it was built from mountain stones, the classes need disks and seats for the students.

The transportation

There is a problem in transportation because the streets and roads are not suitable for the functioning of vehicles. Previously, the population depended on donkeys for most of their transportation within the village or from one village to another, but now cars has been spread in the village of Farriag. To help these cars drive safety, The village need to have safety street.
